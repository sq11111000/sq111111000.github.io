SEEDLIP RANCH WATER

INGREDIENTS

Seedlip Notas de Agave: 2 oz.

Fresh Lime Juice: 1/4 oz

Club Soda: Top

GLASS & GARNISH

Lime wedge

METHOD

Fill a glass with ice.

Add Seedlip Notas de Agave and 1/4 oz of fresh lime juice.

Top with club soda.

Garnish with a lime wedge
